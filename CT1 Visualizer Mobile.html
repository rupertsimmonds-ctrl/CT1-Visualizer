Fees & deposits

  • Agency fee: 5% of contract value, or AED 5,000, whichever is higher, + 5% VAT — payable to betterhomes
  • Security deposit: 5% of annual rent, or AED 10,000, whichever is higher
  • Ejari fee: AED 315 (AED 300 + VAT) — payable to H&H Development LLC
  • DEWA deposit: paid by tenant directly to DEWA. Receipt required before check-in.
  • District cooling (Tabreed) deposit: paid by tenant. Receipt required before check-in.
  • Move-in / lift use deposit: AED 3,000 refundable. Held but not cashed unless damage on move-in.

Tenant requirements

  • Credit score: 650+
  • Cheques: 4 cheques standard. 12 cheques considered for credit score 750+.
  • Bank statements: only required if credit score below 650 or new to country.
  • Third-party payment accepted: EID + passport + visa copy + 3rd-party letter.
  • International transfers: from tenant only — allow 3 – 4 working days for clearance.
  • Pets: small pets only, no dogs (cats fine), subject to NOC.
  • Internet: Etisalat & DU.
  • Parking: allocation per unit type — not all studios get a bay.
